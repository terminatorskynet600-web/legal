Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this Discord bot ("the Bot", "we", "us") handles information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data.

The Bot operates on a stateless basis, meaning:

  • No user data is saved to any database
  • No message content is logged or stored
  • No user IDs, usernames, or Discord data is retained
  • No analytics or tracking is performed

How the Bot Works

When you use a command (such as /mission), the Bot:

  1. Receives your search query
  2. Generates YouTube and GTA Wiki search links based on your input
  3. Sends the response only to you (ephemeral message)
  4. Immediately discards all information - nothing is stored
